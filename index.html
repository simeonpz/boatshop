About Us

Hello! I'm Renan Pinto, the founder of RP Boatworks. Based in Dennis, MA, I’m a devoted husband and father of two wonderful daughters. With over twelve years of experience in the boating industry, I've honed my skills in various aspects of boat maintenance, including painting, fiberglass repairs, woodwork, varnishing and much more.

Driven by a passion for boats and a commitment to high-quality craftsmanship, I decided to launch my own business. My goal is to provide exceptional service and ensure your boat remains in top condition. Whether you need a fresh coat of paint, detailed repairs, or expert woodwork, my team and I are here to help.

Feel free to reach out with any questions or to schedule a service. I look forward to assisting you and ensuring your boating experience is nothing short of excellent.
